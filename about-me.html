Here are some things I like

Mosh, rawk and skank

Music is a huge part of my life. I have been playing guitar since I was 14 years old, and played tenor sax before that. Growing up in the NJ/NY area, I was always into 2000s emo and 90s alternative rock (Neutral Milk Hotel, Smashing Pumpkins, Brand New, Taking Back Sunday, Nirvana, etc.). I joined my first band when I was in college and we played a ton of shows at local bars in the Philadelphia area. It was such a blast having all our friends come out and support us at these shows, and it cemented live music and expression through creativity as staples in my life.

We are going places

I love to travel. There are so many amazing places this world has to offer, places that are incredibly different from the towns and cities I am used to, and I want to get out there and experience these different cultures to see how other people live, what their values are, and what I can learn from them.

Inhale, exhale, breathe

Living in NYC, it's extra important to take some time to get outside of the city and have some nature-time. As my favorite nature boy Ric Flair once said, "Whoo!" Take a ride to the country, get away from the hustle and bustle, and breathe easy for a bit.
